The department of Humanities and Science was established with vision to foster innovative ideas and research among the students and faculty members. It serves as the basis of liberal arts education and provides a platform to explore new horizons in the world of science. The department consists of faculty from the disciplines of Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English. Faculty members possess the ability and talent to be ideal mentors and counsellers to the students. They create the spark in students to learn more and more.
